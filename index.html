PC Game Platforms

    Game platforms are digital distribution services where players can purchase, download, and launch games. These platforms often provide additional services such as multiplayer matchmaking, game updates, achievements, social features, and digital rights management (DRM).
    In this analysis, we are focusing on these three major PC game platforms

  • Steam is the largest and most established platform, known for its vast library, community features, and frequent sales.
  • Epic Games Store is a newer competitor that differentiates itself with exclusive titles and free weekly game offerings.
  • Xbox Games on PC refers to Microsoft's presence in PC gaming offering access to a wide range of games for a subscription fee.

    •     By comparing potential benefits and reducing future costs, gamers can decide which Game platform to purchase a new game from
